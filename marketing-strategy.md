*** Description of the product ***

Fotolokashen: Location Intelligence for Production

Fotolokashen is the premier location intelligence platform built specifically for media production crews. Capture your team's firsthand knowledge—GPS coordinates, photos, production notes, and contacts—and turn it into a searchable, shareable library for your entire crew.

CAPTURE IN THE FIELD
Automatic Logging: Use GPS-tagging to log locations instantly with photos and production notes.

Production Details: Track parking, access points, lighting, and crew setup.

Personalized Data: Add ratings and production dates to stay organized.

SHARE WITH YOUR CREW
Instant Collaboration: Send locations via secure links or directly within the app.

Privacy Controls: Set visibility to private, team-only, or public.

Social Mapping: Follow teammates to see their saved locations on a shared map.

Effortless Briefs: Share location briefs instantly—no more manual copying and pasting.

PLAN SMARTER
Interactive Maps: View your team’s entire location library at a glance.

Powerful Search: Filter by name, area, or one of 15 production-specific types (B-Roll, Interview, Story, etc.).

Team History: See what your teammates have scouted before you even head out.

BUILT FOR PROFESSIONALS
Secure Infrastructure: Features a secure upload pipeline with server-side scanning and conversion.

Seamless Integration: Designed to work alongside Slack, email, and your existing workflow.

Cross-Platform Sync: The iOS companion to the Fotolokashen web platform ensures full team management on the go.

From freelancers to production managers, Fotolokashen replaces scattered notes and forgotten emails with a living library. Stop asking "who knows that location?" and start building collective intelligence.

Every shoot is now shared team knowledge.
